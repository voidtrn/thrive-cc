# Referensi — Sumber Valid & Bisa Diverifikasi

Semua di bawah adalah publikasi **peer-reviewed** atau sumber primer terpercaya. DOI/tautan disertakan agar bisa kamu cek sendiri. Diurutkan berdasarkan peran dalam argumen.

## Sumber primer (fondasi teori)

1. **Dweck, C. S., & Leggett, E. L. (1988).** A social-cognitive approach to motivation and personality. *Psychological Review, 95*(2), 256–273.
   - Model teoretis asli: implicit theories → orientasi tujuan → respons.
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-295X.95.2.256

2. **Dweck, C. S. (2006).** *Mindset: The New Psychology of Success.* Random House.
   - Buku yang mempopulerkan istilah fixed/growth mindset.
   - Katalog penerbit: https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/44330/mindset-by-carol-s-dweck-phd/

3. **Blackwell, L. S., Trzesniewski, K. H., & Dweck, C. S. (2007).** Implicit theories of intelligence predict achievement across an adolescent transition: A longitudinal study and an intervention. *Child Development, 78*(1), 246–263.
   - Bukti longitudinal + intervensi pada remaja.
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1467-8624.2007.00995.x

## Bukti neurosains (mekanisme error-processing)

4. **Moser, J. S., Schroder, H. S., Heeter, C., Moran, T. P., & Lee, Y.-H. (2011).** Mind your errors: Evidence for a neural mechanism linking growth mind-set to adaptive posterror adjustments. *Psychological Science, 22*(12), 1484–1489.
   - EEG: growth mindset ↔ sinyal Pe lebih besar setelah error.
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1177/0956797611419520

5. **Maguire, E. A., dkk. (2000).** Navigation-related structural change in the hippocampi of taxi drivers. *PNAS, 97*(8), 4398–4403.
   - Bukti klasik neuroplastisitas struktural pada manusia dewasa.
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.070039597

## Bukti eksperimen skala besar (efek intervensi)

6. **Yeager, D. S., dkk. (2019).** A national experiment reveals where a growth mindset improves achievement. *Nature, 573*, 364–369.
   - RCT nasional AS; efek nyata untuk siswa berprestasi rendah, bergantung konteks.
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-019-1466-y
   - PubMed: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/31391586/

## Kritik & meta-analisis (batas klaim — WAJIB dibaca untuk deep thinking)

7. **Sisk, V. F., Burgoyne, A. P., Sun, J., Butler, J. L., & Macnamara, B. N. (2018).** To what extent and under which circumstances are growth mind-sets important to academic achievement? Two meta-analyses. *Psychological Science, 29*(4), 549–571.
   - 273 studi / 365.000+ peserta → efek keseluruhan **lemah**; manfaat lebih besar untuk kelompok berisiko.
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1177/0956797617739704
   - PubMed: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/29505339/

8. **Burnette, J. L., O'Boyle, E. H., VanEpps, E. M., Pollack, J. M., & Finkel, E. J. (2013).** Mind-sets matter: A meta-analytic review of implicit theories and self-regulation. *Psychological Bulletin, 139*(3), 655–701.
   - Meta-analisis jalur mediasi (regulasi diri).
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1037/a0029531

9. **Mueller, C. M., & Dweck, C. S. (1998).** Praise for intelligence can undermine children's motivation and performance. *Journal of Personality and Social Psychology, 75*(1), 33–52.
   - Eksperimen pujian trait vs proses.
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1037/0022-3514.75.1.33

10. **Dweck, C. S. (2016).** What having a "growth mindset" actually means. *Harvard Business Review.*
    - Dweck mengoreksi "false growth mindset".
    - Tautan: https://hbr.org/2016/01/what-having-a-growth-mindset-actually-means

## Teori pendukung untuk teknik di file 03

11. **Bandura, A. (1997).** *Self-Efficacy: The Exercise of Control.* Freeman.
    - Sumber keyakinan-diri; mastery experience sebagai yang terkuat.

12. **Ericsson, K. A., Krampe, R. T., & Tesch-Römer, C. (1993).** The role of deliberate practice in the acquisition of expert performance. *Psychological Review, 100*(3), 363–406.
    - Dasar konsep deliberate practice.
    - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-295X.100.3.363

13. **Weiner, B. (1985).** An attributional theory of achievement motivation and emotion. *Psychological Review, 92*(4), 548–573.
    - Dasar retraining atribusi.
    - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-295X.92.4.548

## Cara membaca daftar ini secara kritis

- **No. 1–6** = kenapa growth mindset masuk akal & punya mekanisme.
- **No. 7–10** = kenapa jangan berlebihan mempercayainya; efek kecil & bersyarat.
- **No. 11–13** = fondasi teknik latihan yang membuat perubahan nyata (bukan sekadar afirmasi).

Kalau menemukan konten self-development yang mengklaim mindset sebagai tombol ajaib universal, bandingkan dengan **No. 7 (Sisk dkk., 2018)** dan **No. 6 (Yeager dkk., 2019)** — dua sumber ini paling menahan klaim berlebihan.
