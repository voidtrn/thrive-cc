# BAGIAN 1 — PRONUNCIATION

## SET R1 — Non-Rhoticity: Menghilangkan "r" 🔴

Fitur #1 RP. "r" HANYA diucap kalau diikuti vokal. Lawan insting Indonesia yang baca semua huruf.

### R1.1 Coret "r" yang bisu

Tandai "r" mana yang **diucap** (✓) dan yang **hilang** (✗):

1. ca**r** pa**r**k
2. fou**r** doo**r**s
3. **r**ive**r** **r**uns
4. he**r** fi**r**st bi**r**thday
5. o**r**de**r** mo**r**e wate**r**
6. **r**a**r**e**r**

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R1.1</summary>

1. car ✗ park ✗ → /kɑː pɑːk/
2. four ✗ doors ✗ → /fɔː dɔːz/
3. **r**iver ✓, -er ✗ · **r**uns ✓ → /ˈrɪvə rʌnz/ — /r/ yang kamu dengar di tengah adalah milik `runs` (awal kata), bukan "r" akhir `river` yang hidup lagi. `river` diakhiri konsonan berikutnya (/r/ dari runs) → tetap bisu.
4. her ✗ first ✗ birthday ✗ → /hɜː fɜːst ˈbɜːθdeɪ/ — tiga-tiganya /ɜː/ polos tanpa warna r.
5. order ✗✗ more ✗ water ✗ → /ˈɔːdə mɔː ˈwɔːtə/ — SEMUA r hilang: r di `order` diikuti konsonan /d/, sisanya di akhir kata sebelum konsonan/jeda.
6. rarer /ˈreərə/ — r pertama ✓ (awal kata), r kedua ✓ (diikuti vokal -er), r terakhir ✗ (akhir kata).

Aturan tunggal: **r diucap ⟺ bunyi berikutnya vokal.** Cek per-BUNYI, bukan per-huruf.
</details>

### R1.2 Homophone non-rhotic — kata jadi kembar

Di RP pasangan ini bunyinya **persis sama**. Transkripsikan:

1. source — sauce
2. father — farther
3. pawn — porn
4. caught — court
5. panda — pander

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R1.2</summary>

1. /sɔːs/ — dua-duanya.
2. /ˈfɑːðə/ — dua-duanya.
3. /pɔːn/ · 4. /kɔːt/ · 5. /ˈpændə/
Tes diri: kalau `father` dan `farther` masih beda di mulutmu → r-mu belum hilang. Ini tes non-rhoticity paling jujur.
</details>

### R1.3 Vokal panjang pengganti r 🔴

Huruf "r" yang hilang biasanya "dibayar" dengan vokal panjang. Transkripsikan, fokus vokalnya:

1. nurse, work, learn, girl
2. north, force, born
3. start, heart, calm*
4. near, here — hair, there — tour

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R1.3</summary>

1. /nɜːs/ /wɜːk/ /lɜːn/ /gɜːl/ — semua NURSE = /ɜː/ (bibir netral, JANGAN bulat).
2. /nɔːθ/ /fɔːs/ /bɔːn/ — /ɔː/.
3. /stɑːt/ /hɑːt/ /kɑːm/ — /ɑː/. (*`calm` gak punya r — /ɑː/ datang dari "al"; masuk grup bunyi yang sama.)
4. Diftong centring (khas RP, GenAm gak punya): `near` /nɪə/ · `hair` /heə/ · `tour` /tʊə/ (RP modern sering /tɔː/).
</details>

### R1.4 Linking R & Intrusive R 🟡

"r" mati bisa HIDUP lagi kalau kata berikutnya mulai dengan vokal (linking). RP asli bahkan menyisipkan r yang gak ada di ejaan (intrusive). Tandai di mana [r] muncul:

1. far away
2. mother and father
3. law and order
4. the idea of it
5. media attention
6. for ever and ever

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R1.4</summary>

1. far‿away → [fɑː**r**əˈweɪ] (linking — r ada di ejaan)
2. mother‿and → [ˈmʌðə**r**ən] (linking)
3. law‿and order → [lɔː**r**ənd ˈɔːdə] (**intrusive** — gak ada r di "law"!)
4. idea‿of → [aɪˈdɪə**r**əv] (intrusive)
5. media‿attention → [ˈmiːdiə**r**əˈtenʃən] (intrusive)
6. for‿ever‿and‿ever → [fə**r**evə**r**ənd ˈevə] (linking ×2)

Intrusive R muncul setelah /ɑː ɔː ə/ + vokal. Ini SAH di RP modern — jangan dilawan, justru bikin natural.
</details>

## SET R2 — TRAP–BATH Split 🔴

Kata ber-"a" tertentu pindah dari /æ/ ke /ɑː/. Gak ada aturan ejaan pasti — harus hafal kelompoknya.

### R2.1 Sortir

Masukkan ke kolom /ɑː/ (BATH) atau /æ/ (TRAP):

`bath, math(s), dance, romance, gas, glass, plant, planet, fast, fancy, ask, mass, class, classic, after, laugh, cat, can't, castle, hand`

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R2.1</summary>

**/ɑː/ BATH**: bath, dance, glass, plant, fast, ask, class, after, laugh, can't, castle
**/æ/ TRAP**: maths, romance, gas, planet, fancy, mass, classic, cat, hand

Jebakan favorit:
- `class` /klɑːs/ tapi `classic` /ˈklæsɪk/ — turunan gak ikut!
- `plant` /plɑːnt/ tapi `planet` /ˈplænɪt/
- `dance` /dɑːns/ tapi `romance` /rəʊˈmæns/
- `can't` /kɑːnt/ tapi `can` /kæn/ — beda vokal = pembeda makna penting.

Pola kasar (bukan aturan mutlak): a + /s f θ/ atau a + nasal+konsonan → cenderung /ɑː/.
</details>

### R2.2 Kalimat ranjau

Baca keras, rekam, cek tiap kata bergaris:

1. My **aunt can't dance** at the **castle** — she'd **rather have** a **bath**.
2. **Ask** the **staff** for a **glass** of water **after class**.
3. The **cat sat** on the **grass path**, **half asleep**.

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R2.2</summary>

1. aunt /ɑːnt/ · can't /kɑːnt/ · dance /dɑːns/ · castle /ˈkɑːsəl/ · rather /ˈrɑːðə/ · have /hæv/ (TRAP!) · bath /bɑːθ/
2. ask /ɑːsk/ · staff /stɑːf/ · glass /glɑːs/ · after /ˈɑːftə/ · class /klɑːs/
3. cat /kæt/ · sat /sæt/ (TRAP) · grass /grɑːs/ · path /pɑːθ/ · half /hɑːf/ · asleep /əˈsliːp/ (schwa)

`have` selalu /æ/ meski sekilas mirip pola BATH — frekuensi tinggi, hafalkan sebagai pengecualian.
</details>

## SET R3 — LOT /ɒ/ dan THOUGHT /ɔː/ tetap beda 🔴

GenAm merger `cot=caught`; RP TIDAK. Dua bunyi ini wajib kamu pisah.

### R3.1 Baca berpasangan

1. cot–caught · 2. stock–stalk · 3. odd–awed · 4. wok–walk · 5. body–bawdy

**Tugas**: transkripsi semua, lalu rekam satu napas: *"I caught an odd stock of wok stalks."*

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R3.1</summary>

/kɒt–kɔːt/ · /stɒk–stɔːk/ · /ɒd–ɔːd/ · /wɒk–wɔːk/ · /ˈbɒdi–ˈbɔːdi/
/ɒ/ = pendek, mulut terbuka bulat lemah. /ɔː/ = panjang, bibir lebih maju & bulat.
Kalimat: /aɪ kɔːt ən ɒd stɒk əv wɒk stɔːks/.
</details>

## SET R4 — GOAT /əʊ/ + Smoothing 🟡

### R4.1 GOAT dimulai dari schwa

`go, home, phone, don't, know, over` — titik awal diftong RP = **/ə/** (tengah), bukan /o/. Rekam dua versi: (a) sengaja Amerika /oʊ/, (b) RP /əʊ/. Harus kedengeran beda.

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R4.1</summary>

/gəʊ/ /həʊm/ /fəʊn/ /dəʊnt/ /nəʊ/ /ˈəʊvə/
Trik: mulai dari bunyi "e-pepet" (kayak e di "keras"), meluncur ke /ʊ/. Kalau titik awalmu sudah bulat = masih American.
</details>

### R4.2 Smoothing triphthong 🟢

RP santai "menghaluskan" /aɪə aʊə/ jadi hampir satu suku:

1. fire · 2. hour · 3. flower · 4. science · 5. power

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R4.2</summary>

Formal → smoothed: `fire` /faɪə/ → [faə] · `hour` /aʊə/ → [ɑə~ɑː] · `flower` [flaə] · `science` [saəns] · `power` [paə]
`hour` dan `are` bisa nyaris homophone di RP cepat. Ini polesan — pakai kalau flow-mu sudah stabil.
</details>

## SET R5 — Yod: /j/ dipertahankan 🔴

### R5.1 Baca dengan yod

`tune, news, duty, student, Tuesday, assume, new, reduce`

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R5.1</summary>

/tjuːn/ /njuːz/ /ˈdjuːti/ /ˈstjuːdənt/ /ˈtjuːzdeɪ/ /əˈsjuːm/ /njuː/ /rɪˈdjuːs/
Semua ada /j/ ("y") sebelum /uː/. GenAm buang yod setelah t d n s — RP pertahankan.
</details>

### R5.2 Yod coalescence (RP modern) 🟢

RP kontemporer sering melebur /tj dj/ → /tʃ dʒ/: `tune` [tʃuːn], `duty` [ˈdʒuːti], `Tuesday` [ˈtʃuːzdeɪ]. Dua-duanya sah; yang TIDAK sah buat RP: /tuːn/ tanpa apa-apa (itu American). Rekam tiga versi `Tuesday`: /tj/, /tʃ/, /t/ — pastikan bisa kontrol.

## SET R6 — /t/ Jelas, Tanpa Flap 🔴

### R6.1 T tengah harus tetap /t/

`water, better, city, letter, native, photograph, bottle`

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R6.1</summary>

/ˈwɔːtə/ /ˈbetə/ /ˈsɪti/ /ˈletə/ /ˈneɪtɪv/ — /t/ meledak jelas, JANGAN jadi "wodder/bedder" (flap = American).
`bottle` RP modern boleh glottal [ˈbɒʔl̩] — glottal stop sebelum konsonan silabik/akhir kata makin diterima (`football` [ˈfʊʔbɔːl], `quite good` [kwaɪʔ gʊd]). Yang tetap TABU di RP: glottal di antara vokal (`water` [ˈwɔːʔə] = Cockney/Estuary, bukan RP).
</details>

## SET R7 — Weak Forms & Schwa RP 🟡

### R7.1 Kalimat fungsi lemah

Transkripsi versi UCAPAN NORMAL (bukan citation form):

1. I was going to tell her.
2. There are a lot of them at the door.
3. You should have asked us for some.

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R7.1</summary>

1. /aɪ wəz ˈgəʊɪŋ tə ˈtel ə/ — `was`→/wəz/, `to`→/tə/, `her`→/ə/ (h-dropping kata fungsi sah).
2. /ðər ə ə ˈlɒt əv ðəm ət ðə ˈdɔː/ — `there are a` = tiga schwa beruntun + linking r.
3. /ju ʃəd əv ˈɑːskt əs fə sʌm/ — `should have` → "should've" /ʃəd əv/; `asked` /ɑːskt/ (BATH!).
</details>

## SET R8 — Intonasi RP: Fall-Rise 🟡

RP terkenal dengan **fall-rise** (turun-naik) untuk nuansa "ada tapi..." — sopan, ragu, kontras.

### R8.1 Tandai nada

Tentukan: fall (↘), rise (↗), atau fall-rise (↘↗):

1. "Where do you live?" (pertanyaan WH biasa)
2. "Are you coming?" (yes/no)
3. "I like the design..." (maksudnya: tapi ada yang gak suka)
4. "You could ask David." (saran halus)
5. "It's on the LEFT." (koreksi tegas)

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban R8.1</summary>

1. ↘ fall — WH-question default turun.
2. ↗ rise — yes/no naik.
3. ↘↗ fall-rise — "I ˅like the design" = implikasi "but...". Ini senjata sopan RP: kritik tanpa bilang.
4. ↘↗ fall-rise — saran/tentatif.
5. ↘ fall tajam di LEFT — kontras/koreksi.
</details>

## SET R9 — Shadowing Passage RP 🟢

Baca paragraf ini; tandai sendiri: r bisu, BATH words, linking/intrusive r, weak forms — lalu shadowing rekaman speaker RP (BBC news reader) 5× :

> "After a rather fast start, the older workers can't park their cars near the water. My aunt saw an idea of it on the news last Tuesday, and the media attention was far better than we thought."

<details><summary>▸ Kunci markup (ringkas)</summary>

- r bisu: after, rather, workers, park, cars, near, water, better, far, thought(-r gak ada)
- BATH: after /ˈɑːftə/, rather /ˈrɑːðə/, fast /fɑːst/, can't /kɑːnt/, aunt /ɑːnt/
- Intrusive/linking: idea‿**r**‿of, media‿**r**‿attention, far‿(konsonan, gak linking), saw‿**r**‿an (intrusive!)
- Yod: news /njuːz/, Tuesday /ˈtjuːzdeɪ/
- /t/ jelas: water, better — no flap.
</details>

# BAGIAN 2 — WRITING (British English)

Aksen lisan ↔ konvensi tulisan itu paket. IELTS/CV/email ke institusi UK: konsisten BrE.

## SET W1 — Ejaan British 🔴

### W1.1 Konversi ke ejaan BrE

`color, center, organize*, analyze, catalog, traveling, defense, license (kata benda), practice (kata kerja), gray, program (TV), check (bank), tire (ban), jewelry, skeptical`

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban W1.1</summary>

colour · centre · organise/organize* · analyse · catalogue · travelling · defence · licence (noun) · practise (verb) · grey · programme · cheque · tyre · jewellery · sceptical

Catatan penting:
- *`-ize` sebenarnya SAH di BrE (Oxford spelling) — tapi `-ise` lebih umum; PILIH SATU, konsisten. `analyse` tetap wajib -yse.
- Pasangan lisensi/praktik: **noun pakai c, verb pakai s** → `a licence` / `to license`, `a practice` / `to practise`. (Trik: samakan dengan `advice`/`advise`.)
- Konsonan dobel sebelum akhiran: `travelling, cancelled, modelling` (AmE single l).
- `program` BrE tetap dipakai untuk KOMPUTER; `programme` untuk acara TV/rencana.
</details>

### W1.2 Temukan ejaan campur

Perbaiki paragraf ini supaya 100% BrE:

> "The organization's center recognized that the color of the theater's catalog was a favor to travelers who practiced there, though the defense committee analyzed it skeptically."

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban W1.2</summary>

"The organis**ation's** cent**re** recogni**s**ed that the colo**ur** of the theat**re's** catalog**ue** was a favo**ur** to travel**l**ers who practi**s**ed there, though the defen**c**e committee analy**s**ed it **s**ceptically."
(organisation/organization dua-duanya sah BrE — konsisten saja dengan pilihan -ise/-ize-mu.)
</details>

## SET W2 — Kosakata British 🔴

### W2.1 Terjemahkan AmE → BrE

1. I took the **elevator** to my **apartment** on the **first floor**.
2. Put the **trash** in the **garbage can** on the **sidewalk**.
3. We got **gas** near the **highway** — the **truck** was almost empty.
4. **Fall vacation** starts after the **soccer** game.
5. Wait in **line** at the **drugstore** for **french fries**? (aneh memang)
6. Open the **hood** and check under the **trunk** of the car.

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban W2.1</summary>

1. lift · flat · **first floor AmE = ground floor BrE!** (BrE first floor = lantai 2) — jebakan makna, bukan cuma kata.
2. rubbish · (rubbish) bin · pavement
3. petrol · motorway · lorry
4. autumn holiday · football
5. queue · chemist('s) · chips (dan "chips" AmE = crisps BrE)
6. bonnet · boot

Yang sering bikin salah paham beneran: floor numbering, `chips/crisps`, `pants` (BrE = celana dalam!), `football`.
</details>

## SET W3 — Grammar & Usage British 🟡

### W3.1 Pilih versi BrE

1. (a) Do you have a pen? (b) Have you got a pen?
2. (a) The team are winning. (b) The team is winning.
3. (a) I already ate. (b) I've already eaten.
4. (a) on the weekend (b) at the weekend
5. (a) She's in the hospital. (b) She's in hospital.
6. (a) I'll write you. (b) I'll write to you.
7. (a) learned, spelled, burned (b) learnt, spelt, burnt
8. (a) He's gotten better. (b) He's got better.

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban W3.1</summary>

BrE: 1(b) — `have got` dominan lisan (a juga sah, tapi b lebih khas). 2(a) — kata benda kolektif boleh JAMAK (the team/government/band **are**). 3(b) — present perfect untuk "baru saja" wajib BrE. 4(b) **at** the weekend. 5(b) `in hospital` (sebagai pasien; `in the hospital` = gedungnya). 6(b) write **to** you. 7(b) bentuk -t umum di BrE (dua-duanya sah). 8(b) — **gotten TIDAK dipakai di BrE**, past participle `get` = `got`.
</details>

## SET W4 — Tanda Baca & Format British 🟡

### W4.1 Format ulang gaya BrE

> "Dr. Smith said, "The meeting is on 07/09/2026 at 3:00 PM." Mr. Jones, however, replied that he'd rather meet "sometime in the fall.""

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban W4.1</summary>

> 'Dr Smith said, 'The meeting is on 9 July 2026 at 3 pm.' Mr Jones, however, replied that he'd rather meet 'sometime in the autumn'.'

Aturan BrE:
- **Tanpa titik** setelah singkatan yang berakhir dengan huruf terakhir kata: Dr, Mr, Mrs, St (tapi: Prof. karena terpotong di tengah).
- **Kutip tunggal** utama, ganda untuk kutipan-dalam-kutipan (kebalikan AmE).
- Tanda baca **di luar** tanda kutip kalau bukan bagian kutipan ('...autumn'.).
- Tanggal: **9 July 2026** (hari-bulan-tahun); 07/09 gaya AmE ambigu (dibaca 7 September oleh orang UK!).
- Jam: `3 pm` / `15.00`.
</details>

## SET W5 — Menulis Transkripsi IPA (RP) 🟢

Transkripsikan kalimat berikut penuh (fonemik, dengan weak forms):

1. "The doctor asked her to walk to work."
2. "There's a better idea of it in the news."

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban W5</summary>

1. /ðə ˈdɒktə ˈɑːskt ə tə ˈwɔːk tə ˈwɜːk/ — cek: asked=BATH, walk≠work (/ɔː/ vs /ɜː/), semua r bisu.
2. /ðəz ə ˈbetə**r** aɪˈdɪə**r** əv ɪt ɪn ðə ˈnjuːz/ — linking r (better‿idea: -er + vokal /aɪ/), intrusive r (idea‿of: gak ada r di ejaan), yod di news.
</details>

## SET W6 — Tugas Menulis Bebas + Checklist 🟢

- [ ] Semua ejaan -our/-re/-ise/-ogue konsisten?

- [ ] `I have already submitted...` (present perfect), bukan `I already submitted`?

- [ ] Tidak ada `gotten`?
- [ ] Penutup: `Yours sincerely` (kalau tahu nama) / `Yours faithfully` (Dear Sir/Madam)?
