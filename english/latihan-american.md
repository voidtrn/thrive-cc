# BAGIAN 1 — PRONUNCIATION

## SET A1 — Rhotic R: SEMUA r diucap 🔴

Kabar baik: sesuai insting ejaan Indonesia. Kabar buruk: /r/ American ≠ r getar Indonesia. Lidah **ditarik ke belakang & digulung sedikit (retroflex)** atau dibundel (bunched), TANPA getar, TANPA sentuh langit-langit.

### A1.1 R getar vs r American

Rekam pasangan (Indo vs En), pastikan yang English gak bergetar:

1. "rok" — rock · 2. "raja" — rah · 3. "kertas" — cur · 4. "parkir" — parking

Tes fisik: taruh ujung jari di ujung lidah pas bilang /r/ American — lidah TIDAK boleh menyentuh apa pun di mulut.

### A1.2 Vokal ber-warna-r (r-colored) 🔴

Transkripsikan; fokus /ɝ/ (stressed) vs /ɚ/ (unstressed):

1. bird, nurse, work, learn
2. letter, water, doctor, dollar
3. hurt her first birthday

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban A1.2</summary>

1. /bɝd/ /nɝs/ /wɝk/ /lɝn/ — satu bunyi /ɝ/ untuk SEMUA ejaan ir/ur/or/ear. Bukan "bir-d" — vokal & r melebur jadi satu.
2. /ˈlɛɾɚ/ /ˈwɑɾɚ/ /ˈdɑktɚ/ /ˈdɑlɚ/ — akhiran -er/-or/-ar semua = /ɚ/.
3. /hɝt ɚ fɝst ˈbɝθdeɪ/ — empat warna-r beruntun. Kalau capek rahang = benar; ini otot baru.
</details>

### A1.3 Kluster r brutal 🟢

Satu napas, rekam: *"The northern farmer ordered forty-four more quarters of dark roast."*

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban A1.3</summary>

/ðə ˈnɔrðɚn ˈfɑrmɚ ˈɔrdɚd ˈfɔrɾi fɔr mɔr ˈkwɔrɾɚz əv dɑrk roʊst/
Setiap r kena. `forty` = flap + r-colored [ˈfɔrɾi]. Kalau ada r yang hilang → masih kebawa RP/Indo.
</details>

## SET A2 — T-Flapping: kapan t jadi [ɾ] 🔴

Flap [ɾ] = sentuhan super cepat kayak "r" satu-ketuk Indonesia di "kereta". Aturannya PUNYA SYARAT — bukan semua t.

### A2.1 Flap atau bukan?

Tandai t mana yang di-flap [ɾ], mana yang tetap /t/:

1. wa**t**er · 2. a**tt**ack · 3. ci**t**y · 4. ho**t**el · 5. bu**tt**er · 6. gui**t**ar · 7. mee**t**ing · 8. thir**t**een · 9. la**t**er · 10. car**t**oon

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban A2.1</summary>

Flap [ɾ]: water, city, butter, meeting, later (1,3,5,7,9)
Tetap /t/: attack, hotel, guitar, thirteen, cartoon (2,4,6,8,10)

**Aturan**: flap ⟺ t di antara vokal (atau setelah r) **DAN suku setelahnya TANPA stress**.
`attack` /əˈtæk/ — stress jatuh SETELAH t → t meledak jelas. `hotel` /hoʊˈtɛl/ sama.
Rumus cepat: ˈVtV = flap; VˈtV = /t/ jelas.
</details>

### A2.2 Homophone akibat flap

Di GenAm pasangan ini (nyaris) sama. Baca, lalu transkripsikan:

1. latter — ladder
2. writer — rider
3. metal — medal
4. atom — Adam

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban A2.2</summary>

1. [ˈlæɾɚ] dua-duanya · 3. [ˈmɛɾl̩] · 4. [ˈæɾəm]
2. writer–rider: flap-nya sama [ɾ], tapi banyak speaker beda di VOKAL: `writer` [ˈrʌɪɾɚ] (ai lebih pendek/naik — Canadian raising) vs `rider` [ˈraɪɾɚ]. Level awal: samakan saja, gak masalah.
Konteks yang membedakan — native juga andalkan konteks.
</details>

### A2.3 Flap lintas kata 🟡

Flapping jalan ANTAR kata juga: t akhir + kata berikut mulai vokal.

1. get up · 2. what about it · 3. put it on · 4. a lot of · 5. not at all

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban A2.3</summary>

1. [gɛˈɾʌp] "geddup" · 2. [wʌɾəˈbaʊɾɪt] · 3. [pʊɾɪˈɾɑn] "pudidon" · 4. [əˈlɑɾəv] "aloddov" · 5. [nɑɾəˈɾɔl] "nodadall"
`not at all` = dua flap beruntun — drill favorit. Pelan dulu, naikkan tempo.
</details>

### A2.4 nt → n (winter = winner) 🟢

Banyak speaker GenAm melewati /t/ setelah /n/: `winter` [ˈwɪnɚ], `internet` [ˈɪnɚnɛt], `twenty` [ˈtwɛni], `center` [ˈsɛnɚ]. Opsional — boleh tetap [nt] (lebih jelas, tetap natural). Rekam dua versi `twenty`, pilih satu buat konsisten.

## SET A3 — /æ/ di mana-mana + LOT /ɑ/ + Cot-Caught Merger 🔴

### A3.1 Tanpa TRAP–BATH split

Baca — SEMUA pakai /æ/ (gak ada yang jadi "aa"):

`bath, dance, ask, class, after, laugh, can't, castle, half, fast`

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban A3.1</summary>

/bæθ/ /dæns/ /æsk/ /klæs/ /ˈæftɚ/ /læf/ /kænt/ /ˈkæsl̩/ /hæf/ /fæst/
Enak kan? Gak perlu hafalan split. TAPI: `can't` /kænt/ vs `can` /kən~kæn/ jadi mirip — pembeda utamanya: `can't` stressed + t (sering glottal/unreleased), `can` biasanya weak /kən/.
</details>

### A3.2 LOT = /ɑ/ terbuka lebar

`hot, dog, God, stop, coffee, boss` — mulut TERBUKA lebar tanpa bibir bulat (beda dari RP /ɒ/ yang agak bulat):

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban A3.2</summary>

/hɑt/ /dɔg~dɑg/ /gɑd/ /stɑp/ /ˈkɔfi~ˈkɑfi/ /bɔs~bɑs/
`dog, coffee, boss` grup CLOTH — sebagian speaker /ɔ/, sebagian /ɑ/ (merger). Aman: samakan semua ke /ɑ/.
</details>

### A3.3 Cot–caught merger

Buat mayoritas GenAm modern, pasangan ini SAMA: cot=caught /kɑt/, stock=stalk, don=dawn. **Keputusan praktis: merge saja** — lebih gampang, gak ada penalti. (Bandingkan: di RP ini wajib beda — lihat `latihan-rp.md` SET R3.)

## SET A4 — GOAT /oʊ/ + Yod-Dropping 🔴

### A4.1 GOAT mulai dari /o/ bulat

`go, home, phone, don't, know, over` → /goʊ/ /hoʊm/ /foʊn/ /doʊnt/ /noʊ/ /ˈoʊvɚ/. Titik awal BULAT — jangan schwa (itu RP). Rekam pasangan RP vs GenAm biar sadar bedanya.

### A4.2 Buang yod setelah t d n s 🔴

`tune, news, duty, student, Tuesday, assume, new, reduce`

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban A4.2</summary>

/tun/ /nuz/ /ˈduɾi/ (flap!) /ˈstudn̩t/ /ˈtuzdeɪ/ /əˈsum/ /nu/ /rɪˈdus/
"toon, nooz, doody, stoodent" — /j/ hilang setelah /t d n s/. TAPI yod TETAP setelah bunyi lain: `music` /ˈmjuzɪk/, `cute` /kjut/, `few` /fju/ — jangan overgeneralize jadi "moosic".
</details>

## SET A5 — Dark L di mana-mana 🟡

GenAm pakai L gelap [ɫ] (belakang lidah naik) hampir di SEMUA posisi, termasuk sebelum vokal (RP: clear L sebelum vokal).

1. feel, milk, cold, ball (posisi gelap universal)
2. light, love, believe (GenAm: tetap agak gelap; RP: clear)
3. Drill: *"Little Lily still feels the cold wool blanket."*

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban A5</summary>

1. [fiːɫ] [mɪɫk] [koʊɫd] [bɔɫ~bɑɫ] — lidah belakang naik, bunyi "berat".
2. GenAm boleh [ɫaɪt] — jangan dipaksa jernih kayak "la-li-lu" Indonesia.
3. `little` = [ˈlɪɾɫ̩] — flap + syllabic dark L, kombinasi khas American banget.
</details>

## SET A6 — T akhir: unreleased / glottal 🟡

T di akhir kata/sebelum konsonan biasanya TIDAK diletupkan: `that` [ðæt̚], `cat` [kæt̚], atau glottal `button` [ˈbʌʔn̩], `mountain` [ˈmaʊnʔn̩].

Drill: 1. "That's it." · 2. "I got it." · 3. button, cotton, certain, important

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban A6</summary>

1. [ðæts ɪt̚] · 2. [aɪ ˈgɑɾɪt̚] (flap tengah + unreleased akhir)
3. [ˈbʌʔn̩] [ˈkɑʔn̩] [ˈsɝʔn̩] [ɪmˈpɔrʔn̩t~ɪmˈpɔrtn̩t] — t + syllabic n = glottal stop. "Button" yang diucap "ba-TON" langsung kedengeran non-native.
</details>

## SET A7 — Weak Forms + Reduksi American 🟡

### A7.1 Transkripsi ucapan normal

1. I was going to tell her. ("gonna")
2. What do you want to do? ("whaddaya wanna")
3. You should have asked us.

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban A7.1</summary>

1. /aɪ wəz ˈgənə ˈtɛl ɚ/ — `going to` (niat) → gonna; her → /ɚ/.
2. [ˌwʌɾəjə ˈwɑnə ˈdu] — what+do+you melebur; want to → wanna.
3. /ju ʃʊɾəv ˈæskt əs/ — should have → "should've" dengan flap [ʃʊɾəv]; asked /æskt/ (bukan /ɑːskt/!).
Gonna/wanna = LISAN informal sah; JANGAN ditulis di writing formal.
</details>

## SET A8 — Intonasi GenAm 🟡

Lebih "bergelombang" & range naik-turun lebih lebar dari RP; fall-rise jarang, statement pakai fall mantap.

### A8.1 Tandai nada

1. "Where do you live?" · 2. "Are you serious?" · 3. "That's amazing!" · 4. "I mean, it's fine, I guess..." (ragu)

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban A8.1</summary>

1. ↘ fall. 2. ↗ rise tinggi — range GenAm lebih dramatis dari RP. 3. ↘ dengan puncak pitch tinggi di a-MA-zing. 4. nada datar-melayang (bukan fall-rise RP) + "I mean/I guess" = hedging khas American.
Hindari uptalk (statement naik terus?) — kedengeran gak yakin.
</details>

## SET A9 — Shadowing Passage GenAm 🟢

Tandai sendiri: semua r, flap, yod-drop, reduksi — lalu shadowing speaker GenAm (podcast NPR / Rachel's English) 5×:

> "After a pretty fast start, the older workers can't park their cars near the water. My aunt saw a little video about it on the news last Tuesday, and it got a lot of attention — better than we thought."

<details><summary>▸ Kunci markup (ringkas)</summary>

- r diucap SEMUA: after /ˈæftɚ/, workers /ˈwɝkɚz/, park /pɑrk/, cars /kɑrz/, near /nɪr/, water /ˈwɑɾɚ/, better /ˈbɛɾɚ/
- /æ/: after, fast, can't, aunt /ænt/ (=ant!)
- Flap: pretty [ˈprɪɾi], water, little [ˈlɪɾɫ̩], video, got a lot of [gɑɾəˈlɑɾəv], better
- Yod-drop: news /nuz/, Tuesday /ˈtuzdeɪ/
- about it → [əˈbaʊɾɪt]
</details>

# BAGIAN 2 — WRITING (American English)

## SET W1 — Ejaan American 🔴

### W1.1 Konversi ke ejaan AmE

`colour, centre, organise, analyse, catalogue, travelling, defence, licence, practise, grey, programme, cheque, tyre, jewellery, sceptical`

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban W1.1</summary>

color · center · organize · analyze · catalog · traveling · defense · license · practice · gray · program · check · tire · jewelry · skeptical

Lebih simpel dari BrE:
- `license` & `practice` — **satu ejaan untuk noun DAN verb** (gak ada pasangan c/s).
- Single l: traveling, canceled, modeling.
- -ize WAJIB (analyze, organize) — gak ada opsi -ise.
- `program` untuk semuanya (TV, komputer, rencana).
</details>

### W1.2 Bersihkan paragraf campur

> "The organisation's centre analysed the travellers' behaviour sceptically, then cancelled the programme and offered a defence of its practise."

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban W1.2</summary>

"The organiz**ation's** cent**er** analyz**ed** the travel**ers'** behavi**or** s**k**eptically, then cancel**ed** the program and offered a defen**se** of its practi**ce**."
Bonus: `practise` sebagai NOUN salah bahkan di BrE (noun = practice) — soal ini nguji dua lapis.
</details>

## SET W2 — Kosakata American 🔴

### W2.1 Terjemahkan BrE → AmE

1. My **flat** is on the **ground floor** — take the **lift**.
2. Put the **rubbish** in the **bin** on the **pavement**.
3. We got **petrol** near the **motorway** — the **lorry** was almost empty.
4. **Autumn holiday** starts after the **football** match.
5. **Queue** at the **chemist's**, then get **chips** and **crisps**.
6. Open the **bonnet** and the **boot**.

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban W2.1</summary>

1. apartment · **first floor** (AmE first floor = lantai dasar!) · elevator
2. trash/garbage · trash can/garbage can · sidewalk
3. gas · highway/freeway · truck
4. fall break/vacation · soccer (football AmE = American football)
5. line ("wait in line") · drugstore/pharmacy · fries · chips (BrE crisps = AmE chips — geser rantai!)
6. hood · trunk

Rantai geser paling jebak: BrE chips→AmE fries; BrE crisps→AmE chips. Dan `pants` AmE = celana (aman), BrE = celana dalam.
</details>

## SET W3 — Grammar & Usage American 🟡

### W3.1 Pilih versi AmE

1. (a) Have you got a pen? (b) Do you have a pen?
2. (a) The team are winning. (b) The team is winning.
3. (a) I already ate. (b) I've already eaten.
4. (a) on the weekend (b) at the weekend
5. (a) She's in the hospital. (b) She's in hospital.
6. (a) I'll write you. (b) I'll write to you.
7. (a) learned, spelled, burned (b) learnt, spelt, burnt
8. (a) He's gotten better. (b) He's got better.

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban W3.1</summary>

AmE: 1(b) `do you have`. 2(b) — kolektif = **tunggal** (the team **is**, the government **is**). 3(a) — simple past dengan already/just/yet SAH dan umum di AmE (b juga benar, a khas AmE). 4(a) **on** the weekend. 5(a) `in THE hospital` (selalu pakai the). 6(a) `write you` tanpa to — sah di AmE. 7(a) bentuk -ed. 8(a) — **gotten** hidup di AmE: `has gotten better` (berubah), `has got` = punya. Kebalikannya persis dari kunci BrE di `latihan-rp.md` — bandingkan biar gak ketuker.
</details>

## SET W4 — Tanda Baca & Format American 🟡

### W4.1 Format ulang gaya AmE

> 'Dr Smith said, 'The meeting is on 9 July 2026 at 15.00.' Mr Jones replied that he'd rather meet 'sometime in the autumn'.'

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban W4.1</summary>

> "Dr. Smith said, "The meeting is on July 9, 2026, at 3:00 p.m." Mr. Jones replied that he'd rather meet "sometime in the fall.""

Aturan AmE:
- **Titik** setelah singkatan: Dr., Mr., Mrs., St.
- **Kutip ganda** utama; tunggal untuk kutipan-dalam-kutipan.
- Koma & titik **selalu DI DALAM** tanda kutip ("...in the fall.") — bahkan kalau bukan bagian kutipan. (Tanda tanya/seru: tergantung makna.)
- Tanggal: **July 9, 2026** (bulan-hari-tahun) + koma setelah tahun di tengah kalimat. Numerik: 7/9/2026 = 9 Juli (kebalikan pembacaan UK!).
- Jam: `3:00 p.m.` (titik dua, bukan 15.00).
- Bonus AmE: **Oxford comma** (a, b, and c) lebih standar dipakai.
</details>

## SET W5 — Menulis Transkripsi IPA (GenAm) 🟢

Transkripsikan penuh (dengan flap & reduksi):

1. "The doctor asked her to walk to work."
2. "There's a better video about it on the internet."

<details><summary>▸ Kunci Jawaban W5</summary>

1. /ðə ˈdɑktɚ ˈæskt ɚ tə ˈwɔk tə ˈwɝk/ — asked /æsk/ (bukan BATH), walk vs work: /ɔ(~ɑ)/ vs /ɝ/, semua r hidup.
2. [ðɚz ə ˈbɛɾɚ ˈvɪɾioʊ əˈbaʊɾɪt ɑn ði ˈɪnɚnɛt~ˈɪntɚnɛt] — tiga flap (better, video, about‿it) + opsional nt→n di internet.
</details>

## SET W6 — Tugas Menulis Bebas + Checklist 🟢

Tulis email formal 120–150 kata ke universitas US (menanyakan program master). Audit pakai checklist:

- [ ] Ejaan -or/-er/-ize/-og konsisten? Single l (canceled)?
- [ ] Kosakata AmE (fall semester, program, dorm/residence hall, course)?
- [ ] Kolektif tunggal (`the committee is`)?
- [ ] Dr./Mr. pakai titik; tanggal `July 9, 2026`?
- [ ] `gotten` boleh; `learnt/spelt` jangan?
- [ ] Koma/titik di dalam tanda kutip?
- [ ] Penutup: `Sincerely,` / `Best regards,` (bukan `Yours faithfully` — itu sangat British)?

## ⚖️ Latihan Kontras RP ↔ GenAm (setelah dua-duanya jalan)

Baca kalimat yang sama dua kali — sekali penuh RP, sekali penuh GenAm. Rekam, dengar, pastikan TIDAK bocor:

> "My aunt can't order better water near the new university on Tuesday."

| Kata | RP | GenAm |
|------|----|-------|
| aunt | /ɑːnt/ | /ænt/ |
| can't | /kɑːnt/ | /kænt/ |
| order | /ˈɔːdə/ | /ˈɔrdɚ/ |
| better | /ˈbetə/ | [ˈbɛɾɚ] |
| water | /ˈwɔːtə/ | [ˈwɑɾɚ] |
| near | /nɪə/ | /nɪr/ |
| new | /njuː/ | /nu/ |
| university | /ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəti/ | /ˌjunəˈvɝsəɾi/ |
| Tuesday | /ˈtjuːzdeɪ/ | /ˈtuzdeɪ/ |

Kalau bisa switch bersih dua arah = kamu sudah MENGUASAI fiturnya, bukan sekadar kebiasaan.
