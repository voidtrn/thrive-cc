YOU NEED TO KNOW
All Commercial Area employees are
eligible to work remotely
YOU NEED TO KNOW
Portion of Commercial Area employees work at the office is 50-80% of their time, where it will be breakdown into 3 employee category as follow:

Category 1
Field Forces (REE. SRC, Coach. WEE)
50% On Site 50% Remote Working
Category 2
Zone Team, Analyst/Executive and Finance Team
60% On Site 40% Remote Working
Category 3
Area Team (Manager & Supervisor) Field Trainer, and Regional Manager
60% On Site 40% Remote Working
Detail guideline of Smartwork implementation at Commercial Area Offices can be found in
Commercial NWOW Guidelines
YOU NEED TO KNOW
If employee decide to apply Smartwork, he/she has to complete the Enrollment before [deadline].
  • SG 6 & below
    Arrangement will be communicated by Commercial Management team
  • SG 7 & above
    • Prior the enrollment, you MUST complete the
      Smartwork Capability building module
    • Follow Sampoerna Smartwork enrollment process,
      click here
YOU NEED TO KNOW
Employee is responsible to ensure availability & reachability during remote work, with minimum 7 working hours/day & 40 hours/week as regulated
YOU NEED TO KNOW
Meeting that requires employee to physically attend at certain place within/outside territorial coverage is considered as on-site work. Thus, physical meeting is strongly encouraged to take place when employee is scheduled to work on-site.
YOU NEED TO KNOW
Collaboration week will be arranged to give employees space to engage and collaborate with colleagues & leaders, it is important for you to know further details about this arrangement at your office with your manager.
YOU NEED TO KNOW
People manager is responsible to ensure inclusive & fair Smartwork implementation
EXCEPTION HANDLING
Employee may be required to be present at office during special occasion as per Zone & HQ direction
EXCEPTION HANDLING
Should employee be required to be present at office during remote day/week, it will substitute the on-site day/week as per agreement with people manager (in lieu)
