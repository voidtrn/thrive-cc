Smartwork General Information
Eligibility
Smartwork is eligible for monthly permanent and fixed-term contracted employees who are occupying positions with no requirement to follow specific working hours as driven by business needs and deemed feasible to remote working.
Working Remotely
When working remotely, you are responsible to use proper attire, working in a safe environment that serves the work purpose and ensures productivity, and your obligation of confidentiality in respect of the company’s information remains fully applicable.
Allowed To Work At Any Location
You are allowed to work remotely at the location of your choice within Indonesia and within reasonable commuting distance of the workplace.
Primary and Secondary location
With Smartwork, you will have primary and secondary location. Primary location means the designated office base town location you are assigned to and recorded according to P&C system.

Meanwhile, secondary location is other location where you are required to be presence due to business needs. You are allowed to work in secondary location to fulfill the minimum number of day within a week requiring you to work in designated office, unless you are required to be presence in primary location.
flexible working hours
Smartwork will allow you to have flexible working hours, but you are responsible to ensure the availability and reach-ability during agreed working hours, especially during collaboration hours, which are at 09:00 – 16:00.
