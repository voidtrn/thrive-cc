  • Effective date will be 1st of every month and can not be backdated
STEP 3

Email From Privy
Your registration form will be converted into Consent Form. Privy will send email notification to you to sign the consent form.
STEP 4
Employee Sign
Consent Form On Privy
Consent form will use verified digital signature. If you don’t have Privy account, you need to register private account on Privy before sign the consent form. (Please see Privy Tutorial). You are responsible to ensure your signature and People Manager signature completed in the system. Document on Privy will be expired ... days after you receive notification. If you fail to sign the consent form, you are deemed not to participate the SWA and if you want to continue the registration process, you need to repeat the process from STEP 2.
